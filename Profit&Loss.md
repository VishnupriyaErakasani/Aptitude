
# 📈 PROFIT & LOSS — COMPLETE FORMULA SHEET

---

## 🔑 BASIC DEFINITIONS

Profit = SP − CP  
Loss = CP − SP  

---

## 📌 PERCENTAGE FORMULAS

Profit % = (Profit / CP) × 100  
Loss % = (Loss / CP) × 100  

---

## 🔄 PRICE CONVERSION FORMULAS

SP = CP × (100 + Profit%) / 100  
SP = CP × (100 − Loss%) / 100  

CP = SP × 100 / (100 + Profit%)  
CP = SP × 100 / (100 − Loss%)  

---

## ⚡ DIRECT MULTIPLIERS

### Profit

10% → SP = 1.10 × CP  
20% → SP = 1.20 × CP  
25% → SP = 1.25 × CP  
33⅓% → SP = 4/3 × CP  
50% → SP = 1.50 × CP  
100% → SP = 2 × CP  

### Loss

10% → SP = 0.90 × CP  
20% → SP = 0.80 × CP  
25% → SP = 0.75 × CP  
33⅓% → SP = 2/3 × CP  
50% → SP = 0.50 × CP  

---

## 🧠 RATIO SHORTCUTS

Profit x% → CP : SP = 100 : (100 + x)  
Loss x% → CP : SP = 100 : (100 − x)  

---

## 🔁 SUCCESSIVE PROFIT / LOSS

Net % Change = a + b + (ab / 100)  
(+ for profit, − for loss)

---

## 💡 EQUAL PROFIT & LOSS

Loss % = (x² / 100)  

---

## 🏷️ MARKED PRICE & DISCOUNT

Discount = MP − SP  
Discount % = (Discount / MP) × 100  

---

## 🔄 SUCCESSIVE DISCOUNTS

Net Discount % = a + b − (ab / 100)  

---

## 💰 PROFIT WITH DISCOUNT

Profit % = [(SP − CP) / CP] × 100  

---

## ⚖️ DISHONEST SHOPKEEPER (FALSE WEIGHT)

Profit % = (Extra Weight / Actual Weight) × 100  

---

## 🧪 LOSS RECOVERY FORMULA

Required Profit % = [Loss % / (100 − Loss %)] × 100  

---

## 🎯 SPECIAL VALUES

50% Loss → 100% Profit  
33⅓% Loss → 50% Profit  
25% Loss → 33⅓% Profit  
20% Loss → 25% Profit  
10% Loss → 11.11% Profit  

---

## 📌 GOLDEN RULES

Profit % → Always on CP  
Discount % → Always on MP  
Assume CP = 100 when possible  
Equal Profit & Loss → Always Loss
